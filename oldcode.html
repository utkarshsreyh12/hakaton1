Habitats

Learn about different animal habitats and the animals that live there.

Savannah

The savannah is a grassy plain with scattered trees, home to elephants, lions, giraffes, and zebras.

  • African Elephant
  • Bengal Tiger (rarely)
  • Great Horned Owl (migratory)

Rainforest

Rainforests are dense, warm, and wet forests that are home to diverse species like the red-eyed tree frog and blue morpho butterfly.

  • Red-eyed Tree Frog
  • Blue Morpho Butterfly
  • Giant Panda (temperate rainforest)

Polar

Polar regions are cold and icy, inhabited by animals like the emperor penguin adapted to extreme cold.

  • Emperor Penguin
  • Great Horned Owl (migratory)

About Virtual Zoo

The Virtual Zoo is an interactive web experience designed to educate and entertain users about the amazing animals and habitats found around the world. Our goal is to bring wildlife closer to you through engaging content, sounds, and quizzes.

Created by wildlife enthusiasts and web developers, this platform is perfect for learners of all ages who want to explore nature safely and conveniently.
